Bootcamp Learning Plan


  1. What is your long term goal or career pathway?

    • I am determined to make every effort to secure employment in renowned companies such as Xero, Datacom, Chorus, Spark, One NZ, and 2degrees. My goal is to gain valuable experience from their teams and eventually become a proficient full-stack developer within a decade.

  2. What do you think your biggest strengths and limitations will be in Bootcamp?

    • When faced with numerous distractions, I struggle to maintain focus; however, one of my notable strengths is my unwavering perseverance. I persistently strive towards my objectives without giving up until they are accomplished.

  3. What do you think your biggest non-technical challenge at Bootcamp will be?

    • One of the major hurdles I anticipate encountering during the bootcamp revolves around my ability to effectively communicate and present in front of a group. This particular challenge of public speaking has been an area that I find particularly daunting and will require additional effort and practice to overcome.

  4. What non-technical skills would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?

    • Developing the skill to confidently address and engage a sizable audience is a task I perceive as my most significant challenge within the bootcamp. The prospect of effectively delivering presentations or speeches in front of a group of people is an area that I recognize as requiring substantial improvement and dedicated practice. Acquiring the ability to articulate my thoughts, express ideas, and connect with the audience in a persuasive manner is an essential objective that I am determined to accomplish throughout the course of the bootcamp.

  5. What are your expectations from the Bootcamp team?

    • My expectation of the Bootcamp team is that they consistently provide us with unwavering support and guidance throughout the program. I anticipate that they will be readily available whenever we require assistance, offering their expertise and resources to help us overcome challenges and maximize our learning experience. It is my hope that the team will demonstrate a strong commitment to our growth, fostering a positive and inclusive environment where we feel comfortable seeking their guidance and advice.

  6. What are your expectations of yourself on Bootcamp?

    • In the Bootcamp, I have high expectations of myself to come through and make the most out of this opportunity for growth and learning. To meet these expectations, I will prioritize effective time management by proactively planning ahead and organizing my tasks and responsibilities.

      To ensure I receive timely assistance, I will actively engage with my fellow learners, leveraging the collaborative nature of the Bootcamp to seek help and guidance when required.

      In managing my workload during the program, I am committed to fostering a productive and safe environment for myself and others. This entails demonstrating respect, open communication, and collaboration with fellow learners, facilitators, and industry/community representatives. I will actively listen to others, contribute constructively to discussions, and embrace diverse perspectives. By maintaining a positive and inclusive attitude, I aim to create a conducive learning atmosphere that promotes mutual growth and success.
