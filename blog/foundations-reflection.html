Foundations Reflection

  1. What are three new things you have learnt about yourself and your ego due to the core learning?

    • Through core learning, I have discovered three valuable insights about myself and my ego. Firstly, I have come to understand the significant benefits of meditation as a coping mechanism during times of stress. Secondly, I have realized that taking regular breaks can enhance my productivity levels. Lastly, I have recognized the importance of adhering to a structured learning plan in order to successfully pursue and achieve my career objectives.

  2. What are the role of values, empathy, and self-awareness in learning and programming?

    • Values provide a moral compass, guiding learners and programmers in making ethical decisions and prioritizing principles that align with their work. Empathy fosters understanding and collaboration, enabling learners and programmers to consider diverse perspectives and create inclusive solutions that meet user needs. Self-awareness facilitates personal growth and adaptation, allowing learners and programmers to identify strengths, weaknesses, and areas for improvement to enhance their skills and approach to problem-solving.

  3. What has surprised you the most about the core learning?

    • One surprising aspect of core learning in programming is how it can assist in understanding complex concepts and providing practical coding solutions, even for niche or specialized topics.

  4. What were the most challenging aspects of the core learning?

    • The most challenging aspect of core learning in programming was grasping the intricacies of complex algorithms and understanding how to effectively optimize code for performance.

  5. Why do you think we, a programming school, are spending so much time focusing on core learning in a web development Bootcamp course?

    • To help students develop a strong foundation in fundamental programming concepts, problem-solving skills, and understanding of web technologies, which are crucial for building adaptable web applications and sets them up for long-term success in our programming careers.

  6. Does the time you spent studying core learning here feel like a waste of time? Should you have just used that time to practise programming instead?

    • At first yes, I thought it was a waste of time, but as time goes by, I noticed that it helps me a lot especially when I am stressed. It demonstrates personal growth and understanding of the benefits that core learning brings to programming skills and overall well-being.
